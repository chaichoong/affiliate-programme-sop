1. Programme overview

The Operations Director affiliate programme pays £500 to anyone who introduces a qualifying paying client. Two tracks:

  • Client track: existing Operations Director clients. Auto-approved when added by admin.
  • Public track: non-clients (accountants, consultants, partners). They apply via a public form. Approval is manual by Kevin.

Both tracks share the same payout structure, attribution rules, and dashboard.

2. Commercial terms

StageTriggerAmount
Signup tranche Client's £1,997 setup clears AND 14-day guarantee expires with no refund £250
Retention tranche Client makes their third £247 monthly payment with no chargeback £250
Total per qualified client £500

Attribution window: 60 days from click. Last-click wins.

Payment: BACS or PayPal. Monthly on the 7th. £100 minimum threshold.

3. How tracking works

Six steps from click to conversion:

  1. Affiliate shares https://affiliates.operationsdirector.co.uk/r/CODE
  2. Visitor clicks. App logs the click and sets a 60-day cookie on .operationsdirector.co.uk
  3. App redirects to launch.operationsdirector.co.uk/launch?ref=CODE
  4. Visitor browses, clicks "Get Operations OS", pays via Stripe (unchanged Stripe checkout flow)
  5. Stripe redirects to the thank-you page with ?session_id=cs_xxx. A snippet on that page reads the cookie + session_id and POSTs to the affiliate app
  6. When the Stripe webhook fires checkout.session.completed, the affiliate app matches the session_id to the stored affiliate code and creates a Conversion record
Important: the affiliate scheme does not touch your Stripe checkout, your subscription billing, or your GHL webhooks. All affiliate logic lives in a separate Cloudflare Pages project.

4. Onboarding new affiliates

Adding an existing client as an affiliate

  1. Open the admin panel
  2. In "Add affiliate manually (invited)" enter: full name, email, optional business name, optional custom code
  3. Click Create affiliate
  4. Copy the link that appears (e.g. https://affiliates.operationsdirector.co.uk/r/SARAHJ-422)
  5. Email the affiliate their link with the launch email template from 03-launch-email-and-partner-onepager.md

Processing a public application

  1. When someone applies via the public form, their entry appears at the top of the admin panel under "Pending public applications"
  2. Review their submitted message and business name
  3. Optional: edit the auto-generated code before approving
  4. Click Approve (or Reject)
  5. On approval, email them their link with a welcome message and the partner one-pager attached
Vetting bar for public applicants: must have an audience or network of UK SMEs (5-100 staff). Reject affiliate marketers with no relevant audience, anyone offering to do paid ads on Operations Director brand keywords, or anyone who looks like a competitor.

5. Admin rhythm

Daily (2 mins, optional)

  • Open admin panel
  • Glance at "Conversions" table for any new entries
  • If you see one, the client paid and the affiliate is in line to earn

Weekly (10 mins, every Monday)

  • Process any pending public applications (approve or reject)
  • Send approval emails with the affiliate link
  • Check the leaderboard. Identify top performer of the week. Send a thank-you message
  • Skim any clicks vs conversions to spot under-performers worth coaching

Monthly (30 mins, 7th of every month)

See section 6 below for the full payout process.

Ad-hoc

  • Refund or chargeback notifications from Stripe (see section 7)
  • Affiliate complaints or queries (respond within 1 working day)
  • Removing inactive or abusive affiliates (see section 8)

6. Monthly payout process

On the 7th of every month:

  1. Open the admin panel
  2. Click "Run payout check" (or skip this if your nightly cron is set up via cron-job.org or Make.com)
    • This flips all eligible signup tranches (past 14-day guarantee) from "pending" to "due"
    • This flips all eligible month-3 tranches (3rd monthly payment paid) from "pending" to "due"
  3. Filter the Conversions table to status = qualified
  4. Group "due" payouts by affiliate. For each affiliate:
    • Sum the due tranches
    • If under £100, leave it. It rolls forward
    • If £100+, pay it via BACS (UK bank) or PayPal
  5. Mark each paid payout as "paid" in admin (button on each conversion row)
  6. Send each affiliate a statement email: "You earned £X this month for clients A, B, C. Paid via [BACS/PayPal] today"
VAT note: if the affiliate is VAT registered, they invoice you plus VAT. If not, you pay the £500 net and they self-declare for income tax. Confirm tax setup with each affiliate before the first payment.

7. Refunds, chargebacks, disputes

Refund within the 14-day guarantee

  • Stripe automatically fires charge.refunded webhook
  • Affiliate app marks the conversion as refunded
  • Signup tranche is forfeited (was still pending)
  • No action needed from you. The affiliate is not paid for that referral.

Refund AFTER signup tranche has been paid

  • Affiliate app marks the conversion as refunded and changes payout status to clawed_back
  • Deduct £250 from the affiliate's next monthly payout
  • If they have no upcoming payout, the debt becomes payable. Email them to invoice you back, or write off after 90 days

Chargeback or dispute

  • Same handling as refund. Tranche either forfeited or clawed back depending on whether it was paid yet
  • If chargeback fraud is suspected from the affiliate side, ban the affiliate (set status to banned in admin)

Client cancels before month 3

  • Signup tranche stands. The £250 is paid (client cleared the guarantee window)
  • Month-3 tranche is forfeited. The £250 is not paid

Self-referral attempt

  • Affiliate app automatically blocks attribution when client email = affiliate email. No conversion record is created
  • If you spot a near-miss (different email but obviously same person/business), manually delete the conversion in D1 and email the affiliate to remind them of the rules

8. Pausing or removing an affiliate

To pause (temporary)

  1. Open admin panel
  2. Find the affiliate, set status to paused
  3. Their /r/CODE link still works but clicks won't be logged. Existing conversions still pay out as scheduled

To ban (permanent, abuse or breach of terms)

  1. Set status to banned in admin
  2. Send them written notice via email referencing the breach
  3. Forfeit any unpaid tranches if the breach involved fraud or brand damage. Pay out any earned, properly-attributed tranches if their offence was minor
  4. Do not delete their record. Keep for audit trail

9. Troubleshooting

SymptomLikely causeFix
Affiliate says they clicked their own link to test and no cookie set Browser blocks third-party cookies, or affiliate is in private mode Have them try Chrome incognito with third-party cookies enabled. The cookie is first-party on .operationsdirector.co.uk so it should always work.
Client paid but no conversion appears in admin Either (a) thank-you snippet didn't fire, (b) Stripe webhook isn't reaching affiliate app, (c) attribution window expired Check Stripe webhook logs in Stripe dashboard. Check D1 stripe_session_attributions table for the session_id. If mapping exists but no conversion record, check affiliate app function logs in Cloudflare.
Affiliate didn't get their magic link email Resend API not configured, or email in spam Check Cloudflare Pages → Functions → Real-time logs for the magic link URL printed there. Forward manually. Or set up Resend properly.
Admin panel asks for password every page load Basic Auth is per-session Expected behaviour. Stay signed in for the session.
Public application form returns 500 error Possible: malformed input. Likely: D1 binding misconfigured Cloudflare dashboard → od-affiliates → Settings → Bindings → confirm DB binding = od-affiliates.
